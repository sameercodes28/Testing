Villa Sommarro, Skövde

2024 • Nybyggnad • Villaritning • Skövde, Västra Götaland

Client Goal

To create a modern, sustainable family home that feels integrated with the natural landscape of Västra Götaland.

Architectural Solution

An open-plan villa designed around the sun's path, utilizing locally-sourced timber and large glass partitions to connect indoor and outdoor spaces.

Key Features

  • +180m² of living space
  • 65% reduction in energy needs
  • Locally sourced Skaraborg timber
  • Floor-to-ceiling panoramic windows

A Vision of Sustainable Living

The journey for Villa Sommarro began with a deep analysis of the site's unique character. The client's vision was a home that was not just built on the land, but a part of it. We studied the sun's path, wind patterns, and the surrounding forest to inform a design that maximizes natural light and minimizes environmental impact.

Harmonizing Structure and Nature

The core of the design is its material honesty. We chose a facade of locally-sourced, untreated timber that will develop a beautiful silver patina over time, allowing the villa to merge gracefully with its woodland setting. The building's orientation and deep eaves are a direct response to the Nordic climate, providing passive heating in winter and cooling shade in summer.

An Interior Bathed in Light

Inside, the open-plan living space is the heart of the home. Floor-to-ceiling glass partitions erase the boundary between inside and out, making the surrounding nature a permanent feature of the interior design. The layout is designed for modern family life, with flexible zones that can adapt as the family's needs evolve.
