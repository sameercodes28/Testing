Renovering med Vätternutsikt

2024• Renovering • Tillbyggnad• Vättern, Västra Götaland

The Vision

This project began not with a blueprint, but with a feeling. The clients wanted to transform their dated 1960s house into a sanctuary that honored its spectacular view of Lake Vättern. The challenge was to open up a compartmentalized layout while respecting the home's original character.

The Transformation

By removing key interior walls, we created a single, light-filled space that combines the kitchen, dining, and living areas. Floor-to-ceiling glass doors now frame the lake, turning the view from a simple backdrop into the heart of the home. A new, seamless extension houses a private master suite, creating a dialogue between the original structure and its modern counterpart.

The Result

The home is reborn. It is a space that breathes, connected to the shifting light and seasons of the lake. The renovation improved energy efficiency by over 70%, but more importantly, it created a home that enhances daily life. It proves that thoughtful architecture can bridge the past and the future, creating something both timeless and new.
