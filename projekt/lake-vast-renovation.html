Renovering med Vätternutsikt

2024 • Renovering • Tillbyggnad • Vättern, Västra Götaland

Client Goal

To transform a dated 1960s house into a modern home, maximizing the stunning lake views while respecting the original architecture.

Architectural Solution

A complete renovation to create an open-plan living space, plus a seamless extension for a new master suite with its own private lake view.

Key Features

  • Open-plan social area of +60m²
  • Seamless indoor-outdoor flow
  • 70% improvement in energy efficiency
  • New master suite with spa-like bathroom

Bringing a Classic into the Future

This project was a complete transformation of a classic 1960s house with one of the most beautiful views in Västra Götaland. The challenge was to modernize the structure, improve its energy efficiency, and open up the layout to the panoramic view of Lake Vättern, all while honoring the home's original character.

An Extension that Complements

A key part of the project was a sensitive extension to house a new master suite. We designed the extension to feel like a natural part of the original building, using similar roof lines but with a modern timber facade. This creates a subtle dialogue between old and new. The new suite now enjoys its own private, perfectly framed view of the lake.

A Focus on Light and Connection

By removing interior walls and installing large, triple-glazed sliding doors, we dissolved the boundary between the living space and the expansive terrace. The result is a home that feels larger, brighter, and constantly connected to the shifting light and seasons of the lake.
