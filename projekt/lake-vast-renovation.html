Renovering med Vätternutsikt

2024• Renovering • Tillbyggnad• Vättern, Västra Götaland

The Challenge

To breathe new life into a classic 1960s house, transforming its closed-off layout into an open, light-filled home that fully embraces its spectacular lakeside setting.

År:
2024
Typ:
Renovering, Tillbyggnad
Plats:
Vättern, Västra Götaland
Tjänster:
Arkitektritning, Bygglovsritningar, Projektering

Opened the Core

Removed interior walls to create a 60m² social hub, combining kitchen, dining, and living areas with an uninterrupted view of the lake.

Added a Modern Wing

Designed a seamless extension for a private master suite, giving it a secluded view and a direct connection to the surrounding nature.

Bridged Inside and Out

Installed floor-to-ceiling glass doors that slide away, dissolving the barrier between the interior and a new, expansive terrace.

Boosted Efficiency

Upgraded the entire building envelope and installed modern systems, resulting in a 70% improvement in energy efficiency.

The Result

The result is a home reborn. It retains the soul of its 1960s origins but now functions for modern life, with spaces that are both expansive and intimate. The connection to the lake is no longer just a view; it's an integral part of the daily living experience. This project stands as a testament to how thoughtful renovation can honor the past while designing for the future.
