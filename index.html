About

Karin Gunnerek is a Stockholm-based architect renowned for her minimalist aesthetic and commitment to sustainable, human-centric design. After graduating with honors from the KTH School of Architecture, Karin founded her practice in 2015 with a vision to create spaces that blend functionality with quiet elegance.

Her work has been featured in numerous design publications and is celebrated for its thoughtful use of natural light and materials. She believes that great architecture is a dialogue between the environment, the structure, and the inhabitants.
